# Business Model Layer
## Kako klijenti prelaze kroz tier-ove + šta platforme stvarno podržavaju

**Cilj dokumenta:** Razumeti kako se danas zaista prodaje 1-on-1 coaching online. Ne samo "imam app gde primam klijente" — nego ceo **funnel od stranca do high-ticket klijenta**, i šta od toga platforme prave automatski, a šta trener mora ručno.

**Ključan uvid odmah na početku:** Većina platformi koje smo analizirali su tehnički "delivery tools" — tvoj app gde klijent vidi workout. Ali **funnel layer** (kako stranac postaje klijent) je gotovo uvek odvojen — ClickFunnels, Stan Store, ManyChat, Calendly, Stripe, ručno. Ovo je velika prilika koju većina coach app-ova ignoriše.

---

## I. PRVO — Razumeti TIPOVE coaching ponude

Ne postoji "samo 1-on-1". Postoji **value ladder** sa različitim tipovima ponude. Većina trenera koristi 3-5 različitih tipova istovremeno.

### Industry standard tier sistem:

| Tier | Cena (US/UK) | Tip ponude | Touch | Margins | Vreme trenera |
|------|--------------|------------|-------|---------|---------------|
| **Free / Lead Magnet** | $0 | PDF, mini-course, free sample workout | None | Loss leader | 0 (jednom napisano) |
| **Tripwire** | $7-$47 | 7-day workout PDF, 30-day challenge | None | High % | 0 (passive) |
| **Low-Touch Self-Serve** | $50-$200/mo | App pristup + pre-built program | Minimal | Vrlo visok | 0 (passive) |
| **Group / Mid-Touch** | $200-$700/mo | Group challenge, autoflow, group coaching | Limited | Visok | 2-5h/sedmica |
| **1-on-1 Standard** | $500-$1500/mo | Custom programs, weekly check-ins, async messaging | High | Srednji | 5-10h/sedmica |
| **High-Ticket 1-on-1** | $1500-$5000/mo | Custom + weekly calls + priority chat + nutrition | Very High | Niži (vreme intenzivno) | 10-20h/sedmica |
| **VIP / Concierge** | $5000-$10000+ | Daily access, 24/7, in-person + virtual hybrid | Maximum | Najniži po dolaru, najveći po klijentu | 20-40h/klijent |

**Industry citat:**
> "Low-ticket: Downloadable workout plan or app access ($50-$150). Mid-ticket: Monthly coaching with videos, check-ins, and progress tracking ($300-$700/month). **High-ticket: High-performance program with personalized training, nutrition, and weekly one-on-one calls ($3,000-$6,000)**" — Entrepreneurs HQ Coaching Models 2026

## II. KAKO klijent ZAPRAVO putuje kroz funnel

### Najčešći put (consumer journey) za **fitness coaching klijenta** danas:

```
1. STRANAC vidi tvoj reel/post na Instagramu/TikToku
   ↓
2. Klikne na link u biou → landing page
   ↓
3. Daje email za "FREE: 7-Day Beginner Workout PDF"
   ↓
4. Email sequence (5-7 emails) preko ConvertKit/Mailchimp
   ↓
5. Email #3 nudi $27 "30-Day Transformation Challenge" (TRIPWIRE)
   ↓
6. Po kupovini ulazi u tvoj app sa pre-built challenge programom
   ↓
7. Tokom challenge-a (kroz dnevne push notif. i app) vidi tvoj content
   ↓
8. Day 25 dobija ponudu: "$197 Self-Serve 12-Week Program" (LOW TOUCH)
   ↓
9. Ako kupi → ulazi u app sa template programom + group access
   ↓
10. Pri kraju 12 nedelja: "Want personal coaching? Book a Strategy Call"
    ↓
11. Free 30-min call (Calendly) → discovery → pitch
    ↓
12. Ako konvertuje → $1500/mo 1-on-1 coaching (HIGH TICKET)
```

**Statistika konverzije:**
- ~5% **lead → tripwire** ($0 → $27)
- ~10-15% **tripwire → low-touch** ($27 → $197)
- ~20-30% **low-touch → high-touch** ($197 → $1500/mo)
- ~3-7% **landing page → high-ticket call**

Najveći uspeh: **ne mora svako biti klijent.** Pažljivo odbiraš ko ti odgovara.

## IV. DUBINSKA ANALIZA — Kako svaka platforma misli o tier sistemu

### 🏆 PT DISTINCTION — Najbolji "Multi-tier u jednom alatu"

PT Distinction je **najsofisticiraniji** kad pričamo o multi-tier coaching modelu. Razlozi:

#### Off-the-Shelf Packages (Ovo je najjača ponuda u industriji)

> "In PT Distinction, you can set up **pre-made packages to give away or sell on your website.** Now, this is brilliant! Things like **free trials, sample programs, challenges, or any package that you want to sell to a large number of people** can have an automated sign up."

**Kako to praktično radi:**
1. Trener pravi paket (npr. "30-Day Beginner Strength" za $97)
2. Sistem generiše embed code → trener stavlja na svoj site
3. Klijent kupuje preko PayPal embed-a
4. **Automatski** se pravi nalog u PT Distinction
5. Klijent dobija pristup app-u sa **tačno** programom koji kupio
6. Trener ne radi ništa — pasivni dohodak

> "It will give the client all the coaching, assessments, emails, and anything else you created. After this, they'll be able to access everything through your website and apps. **There's very little that you have to do here, other than creating the programs. Just take a back seat and watch the money come in.**"

#### Multiple Coaching Tiers istovremeno
> "I can offer **personal one-to-one, group training or a combination of online training and personal training.**"

> "**Then there is the ability to create totally automated packages and sell them to the world while I sit back and relax... True passive income! I have so far got 2 automated programs, one for coaching cricket fast bowlers and another for weight loss. They are both low cost and can help almost anyone in the world.** This is great for me as my face to face PT prices are £120 per hour so I can now help anyone and not just the rich!"

**Šta je ovo strateški:** Trener ima **3 revenue stream-a** istovremeno:
- £120/h face-to-face (high touch, premium)
- £20-50 automated programs (low touch, scale)
- £80-150/mo middle (mix)

#### Mane:
- Steep learning curve
- "**The concepts and tools available on PT Distinction are difficult to master**"
- Manje user-friendly za klijenta vs. Hevy/Everfit

### 🏆 EVERFIT — Najjača "Autoflow" (Group Automation)

Everfit je **kralj group/challenge automation**. Njihov **Autoflow** je glavni razlog zašto trener uzima Everfit umesto Trainerize.

#### Šta je Autoflow:
> "If you add a client to an active Autoflow, they will automatically receive an automated delivery of training materials (workouts, tasks, messages, announcements, leaderboard…)"

> "There are two types of Autoflows: **'Exact Date' and 'By Day Sequence'.** With the **'Exact Date'** Autoflow, clients will participate in group training with the same schedule synced across the Autoflow. With the **'By Day Sequence'** Autoflow, all you need to do is set up a schedule of programming once, and you can reuse the same sequence for more clients even if they start on different dates."

**Praktičan use case:**
- Trener pravi "30-Day Summer Shred Challenge"
- Postavi 30 dana workouts, automated check-in messages, leaderboard, progress photo prompts
- Postavlja na svoj site
- 50 ljudi se prijavi → svi automatski dobijaju isti flow
- Trener radi **0 minuta** za delivery — samo gleda leaderboard
- Day 28 šalje broadcast: "Want to continue with personal coaching? Reply YES"

#### Free Trial Packages (Everfit, May 2023)
> "Unlocking new clients, building trust, and skyrocketing conversions is essential for any fitness coach. That's why we've introduced our new feature: the **Free Trial Package**. By offering free trials for your packages, you can give potential clients a taste of your transformative training programs without any reason to hesitate."

**Praktično:** Klijent dobija 7-day FREE access. Posle se naplata automatski uključi (kao Netflix). Za novu akviziciju zlatno.

#### Šta Everfit još nudi:
- One-time payments
- Recurring subscriptions
- Discount codes
- HSA/FSA tax-deductible payments (US)
- Custom branded apps (Studio plan)

#### Mane:
- Add-on model **skup**: Autoflow je separately billed (paying per active client)
- Bez landing page builder-a
- Bez email automation izvan platforme

Mnogi treneri još rade ovako. Plus jednostavnost. Minus: nema struktura, nema klijent app.

Trend kod Gen Z trenera. Notion za programe, Stan Store za prodaju, Slack za komunikaciju. **Hipster** stack.

## VI. KONKRETAN FUNNEL — Kako klijent danas ide kroz tier-ove

Uzmimo **realistic primer**: Trener sa 200 followera na IG-u, hoće da skalira na 30 klijenata mesečno.

### Faza 1: AKVIZICIJA (Stranac → Lead)

**Kanal:** Instagram Reels
**Konverzija:** 1000 views → 50 link clicks → 15 email signups

**Tooling:**
- Reel: Mobitel, capcut, IG
- Link in bio: **Stan Store** ($29/mo) ili Linktree
- Lead magnet: PDF "5 Workouts Without Equipment"
- Email capture: ConvertKit ($29/mo) ili Mailchimp (free do 500)

**Šta coaching platform tu radi:** **NIŠTA.** Niko od 7 platformi nema landing page builder + lead magnet capture za novi lead.

**Tvoja prilika:** Built-in landing page builder za fitness niše. Takav alat ne postoji.

---

### Faza 2: KONVERZIJA Lead → Tripwire ($0 → $27)

**Email sequence (Day 1-7):**
- Day 1: "Welcome + here's your free workout"
- Day 3: "How to actually do it (form check video)"
- Day 5: "Why most beginners fail"
- Day 7: "**Limited offer: $27 30-Day Strength Challenge**"

**Tooling:**
- Email automation: **ConvertKit** ($29-99/mo)
- Tripwire payment: **Stripe** + simple landing page
- App access delivery: **Manual** — trener šalje klijentu invite link

**Šta coaching platform tu radi:** Everfit i PT Distinction omogućavaju **embed** sign-up form na sopstvenom site-u koji direktno kreira nalog. Ovo je **odlično**, jer ukida manual rad.

> "Once you've completed your package and saved it, **the code will appear for you to copy and paste into your website. This will make the sign-up form magically appear on your website for clients to use.**" — PT Distinction

**Tvoja prilika:** Native Stripe checkout integration koji **automatski** kreira nalog + assignuje program. Već PT Distinction radi ovo.

Klijent plati $27. Sada šta?

**Idealno:**
1. Email confirmation sa app download link

### Faza 4: TIER UPGRADE — Tripwire → Low-Touch ($27 → $197)

**Ovo je gde sve platforme PUKNU.**

Klijent koji je završio $27 challenge želi da nastavi → **kako mu nuditi $197 12-week program iz iste app?**

**Ne postoji elegantno rešenje:**
- Trainerize: Trener manualno mora kreirati novi product, poslati linku, čekati da klijent kupi, manualno premestiti u novu program.
- Everfit: Slično. Manualno.
- PT Distinction: **Najbolje** — multiple packages u istom client profile-u, ali još uvek manual handoff.

**Tvoja prilika:** **In-app upgrade flow.** Klijent vidi "Continue your journey" CTA u app-u, klikne, plaća kroz built-in Stripe, automatski dobija novi program assigned. **Niko ovo ne radi.**

### Faza 5: TIER UPGRADE — Low-Touch → 1-on-1 ($197 → $1500/mo)

**Kako je danas:**
1. Trener primeti da je klijent **angažovan** (loguje 80%+ workouts)
2. Šalje DM: "Vidim da napreduješ! Hoćeš li voditi razgovor o personal coaching-u?"
3. Klijent klikne **Calendly** link
4. Free 30-min discovery call
5. Pitch + close
6. Posle close-a: trener prebaci klijenta u novi tier (manual upgrade u app-u)
7. Stripe subscription se prebacuje od $197/quarter na $1500/mo

**Šta tu boli:**
- Discovery call booking je external (Calendly $12/mo)
- Strategy session pitch je manual
- Klijentska baza nije u istoj bazi (CRM odvojen)
- Switch klijenta iz "low-touch group" u "1-on-1" se radi manualno
- Stripe subscription change se radi manualno

**Tvoja prilika:** Native discovery call booking (kao Calendly) + automated tier upgrade flow + Stripe subscription change u 1 click.

## VII. ŠTA NIKAKO NE PRAVIŠ JEDNAKO ZA SVE TIPOVE KLIJENATA

Ključ: **svaki tier zaslužuje različit klijent experience**.

### Greška većine platformi: "One-size-fits-all interface"

Trainerize: Klijent koji plaća $30/mo za self-serve program i klijent koji plaća $1500/mo za 1-on-1 vide **isti app**. To je strateška greška.

### Šta je pravilno:

#### Tripwire klijent ($27) treba da vidi:
- Samo svoj program (no messaging, no extra tabs)
- "Upgrade" CTA
- Jednostavan workout logging
- Bez chat-a sa trenerom (low margin)

#### Low-touch klijent ($197) treba da vidi:
- Program
- Group chat (asynchronous, ne trener-to-1)
- Weekly check-in form (auto)
- Habit tracker
- "Upgrade to 1-on-1" CTA

#### 1-on-1 klijent ($1500/mo) treba da vidi:
- Sve gore +
- Direktan chat sa trenerom (fast response)
- Custom program
- Weekly video check-ins
- Custom macros
- Personal video tutorials

#### High-Ticket klijent ($3000/mo) treba da vidi:
- Sve gore +
- VIP badge u app-u (psychological value)
- Direct phone access
- 24/7 messaging priority
- Special "VIP Lounge" content
- Quarterly assessments

**Niko od 7 platformi ne radi ovo elegantno.** Sve forsiraju "uniform client experience".

## VIII. FUNNEL TYPES — Šta tržište koristi

### Funnel Type 1: **Challenge Funnel** (najpopularniji u fitness niši)

> "A Challenge Funnel is a **short, high-value experience (usually 5-10 days) designed to get the audience a specific quick win** related to your core product. The primary goal is to **build deep trust and demonstrate your expertise by delivering tangible results before asking for a significant financial commitment.**"

**Praktično:**
- 7-day "Morning Energy Boost Challenge" za $0 ili $7
- Day 1-5: Daily workouts + content
- Day 6-7: Pitch za $497 12-week program
- Conversion: 10-15% iz challenge-a u paid

**Šta ti treba:** Autoflow funkcionalnost. **Everfit** je naj-jak ovde.

```
Free Lead Magnet ($0) 
  → Tripwire ($7-$47)
```

> "Once someone buys from you, they cross a psychological threshold. **They're no longer just a subscriber. They're a buyer. And buyers are 10x more likely to purchase from you again.**"

**Šta ti treba:**

## X. STRATEŠKI ZAKLJUČCI — Šta to znači za tvoju app

### Glavni nalaz #1: **Niko ne pokriva ceo funnel**

7 glavnih platformi su sve **delivery-first.** Funnel layer (akvizicija + tripwire + tier upgrade) je secondary ili nepostojeći.

**Tvoja prilika:** Built-in funnel layer. Kao **Stan Store** + **ConvertKit** + **Calendly** + **Trainerize** = jedan alat.

---

### Glavni nalaz #2: **Klijenti različitih tier-ova zaslužuju različit experience**

Niko ne pravi tier-aware UI. Trener mora **manualno** da konfiguriše šta koji klijent vidi.

**Tvoja prilika:** Native tier system. Trener bira tier (Tripwire / Self-Serve / Group / 1-on-1 / VIP), klijent automatski dobija različit UI/access nivo. **Tier upgrade u 1 click** + automated Stripe subscription change.

---

### Glavni nalaz #3: **Tier upgrade nije podržan**

Najveći business problem: kako klijent prelazi iz $27 challenge-a u $1500/mo coaching, **u istom app-u**, bez drama.

**Tvoja prilika:** "Smart Upgrade Flow":
1. Klijent vidi "Upgrade to 1-on-1" banner u app-u
2. Klikne → vidi pricing options
3. Plati kroz built-in Stripe (subscription change automatic)
4. Trener dobija notifikaciju "Klijent X upgrade-ovao na 1-on-1"
5. App prebacuje klijenta u novi UI tier (sa novim features)
6. Trener radi onboarding kao i obično

**Niko ovo ne radi.** Ovo bi te pravilo unique.

---

### Glavni nalaz #4: **Group + Challenge automation je table stakes**

Everfit Autoflow je standard. Bez toga **ne možeš** ozbiljno na tržište.

**Tvoja prilika:** Autoflow-style funkcionalnost po default-u, ne kao add-on. Free trial packages takođe.

---

### Glavni nalaz #5: **Discovery call booking treba da je native**

Trenutni stack: Calendly + Stripe + email = 3 alata. Trebaće biti 1.

**Tvoja prilika:** Native booking + payment + intake form + automatic add to coaching tier post-purchase.

---

### Glavni nalaz #6: **Email automation je separately**

Trenutno trener koristi ConvertKit ili Mailchimp odvojeno. To je $29-99/mo extra.

**Tvoja prilika:** Native email/SMS automation za:
- Lead magnet delivery
- Drip email sequence
- Tripwire promotion
- Discovery call follow-up
- Tier upgrade prompts
- Re-engagement of inactive clients

## XIV. CONCRETE EXAMPLE — Kako bi se trener postavio na tvoju platformu

**Sara, ITV PT, 27 godina, IG: 5K followers, hoće da skalira do 30 1-on-1 klijenata + 200 self-serve klijenata.**

### Setup u tvojoj app-u (15-30 min):

1. **Brand profile:** Logo, boje, custom domain
2. **Tier configuration:**
   - Tier 1: "Sara's 7-Day Quick Start" — $0 (lead magnet)
   - Tier 2: "30-Day Strength Challenge" — $47 (tripwire)
   - Tier 3: "12-Week Self-Paced Transformation" — $197 (low-touch)
   - Tier 4: "Sara's Personal Coaching" — $497/mo (1-on-1 standard)
   - Tier 5: "Sara's VIP Package" — $1497/mo (high-ticket)

3. **Funnel automation:**
   - Stranac dobija PDF (Tier 1) za email
   - 7-day email sequence
   - Day 7: pitch za Tier 2 ($47)
   - Tier 2 klijenti dobijaju 30-day challenge (autoflow)
   - Day 25: pitch za Tier 3 ($197)
   - Tier 3 klijenti dobijaju group + program
   - Day 60: "Want personal coaching?" → Calendly booking
   - Discovery call → close → Tier 4 ili Tier 5

4. **Klijent flow:**
   - Tier 1-2 klijenti: minimal UI, samo program, no chat
   - Tier 3 klijenti: program + group chat + weekly check-ins
   - Tier 4 klijenti: full custom + 1-on-1 chat + video calls
   - Tier 5 klijenti: sve gore + VIP badge + 24/7 priority

5. **Sara's dashboard:**
   - Total active klijenti: 230
   - MRR: $497 × 30 = $14,910 (Tier 4) + $197 × 200 / 3 = $13,133 (Tier 3) = **$28,043/mo**
   - Tripwire revenue (Tier 2): $47 × 50/mo = $2,350
   - Total: **~$30,000/mo**

**To je realan business build na tvojoj platformi. Trenutno to zahteva 5-7 tools.**
